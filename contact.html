Frequently Asked

Which class level is right for me?

If you've never picked up needles or sat at a sewing machine, start with Little Leaguer. If you have some experience but want to grow, try All-Star. GOAT is for folks who are already very comfortable and want a real challenge.

Do I need to bring anything?

Nope — all supplies are included in your subscription. Just bring yourself and a willingness to try.

Can I try before I subscribe?

Send us an email and we'll talk through the options. We want you to feel good about your class before you commit.

What if I miss a class?

Life happens. Your subscription gets you 4 sessions per month — reach out if you have questions about makeup options.
